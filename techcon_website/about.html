History of TechCon

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the mission of bringing together leading innovators and thinkers in the tech industry. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most important conferences for tech professionals and enthusiasts worldwide.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire and educate technology professionals by providing them with the resources and networks they need to create groundbreaking innovations. TechCon promotes diversity, inclusivity, and progress within the tech industry.

Notable Past Speakers

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, the visionary behind companies like Tesla and SpaceX, shared his thoughts on the future of technology and space exploration during his keynote speech in 2021.

Sheryl Sandberg

In 2019, Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Meta (formerly Facebook), talked about the importance of leadership and technology in shaping the future.
